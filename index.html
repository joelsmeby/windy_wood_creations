About

About

Hi, I'm Joel Smeby — an engineer, maker, and designer based in the Columbia River Gorge, Oregon. I started Windy Wood Creations, LLC to help people find their way without having to constantly pull out their devices and check a map. The end product is a durable, well-designed outdoor sign that feels authentic to its environment, whether that's the forest, beach, or local community trail system.

Everything is built locally in Oregon with an emphasis on craftsmanship, consistency, and clean design.
